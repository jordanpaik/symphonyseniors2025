you are in God's hands!

“He has made everything beautiful in its time. Also, he has put eternity into man's heart, yet so that he cannot find out what God has done from the beginning to the end."
Ecclesiastes 3:11

In seasons of waiting, confusion, or desperation, this verse reminds me that God's plan is greater than anything I'm facing right now. I'm not meant to have it all figured out and I don't have to bear the weight of knowing everything. We are simply called to trust in the one who does because He promises us a future that is beautiful and beyond our understanding.

~ Aaron Kim, Northeastern ~

“For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans for welfare[a] and not for evil, to give you a future and a hope."
Jeremiah 29:11

In times of uncertainty/discouragement, this verse is a reminder that God has a promisingly bright and hopeful future planned for you! Your life is not meaningless/forgotten. Remember that God is not absent as He is always working. He has not and will not give up on you. Even if you currently don't understand it, trust in Him and He will lead you into His perfect plan. you got this i believe in you! :]

~ Soo Jung, Boston University ~
