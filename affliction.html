“...even the darkness is not dark to you; the night is bright as the day. for darkness is as light with you. for you formed my inward parts; you knitted me together in my mother's womb. I praise you, for I am fearfully and wonderfully made...”

~ Jordan Paik, Boston University ~

“Peace I leave with you; my peace I give to you. Not as the world gives do I give to you. Let not your hearts be troubled, neither let them be afraid.”
John 14:27

This verse really spoke to me as I was wrapping up the semester and wondering where I was headed. The semester ended well despite the chaos and I think this verse really reminds me of the peace that God gives us that is given freely and that lasts. I think it is easy to get caught up in things that are temporal and forget that God's peace which lasts forever is with each of us and that is where our strength and comfort should come from.

~ Lois Nfor, Northeastern ~

“I have said these things to you, that in me you may have peace. In the world you will have tribulation. But take heart; I have overcome the world.”
John 16:33

When I was going through a really terrible time in college, this verse was such an encouragement for me. I thought there was something I was doing wrong that was bringing trouble, and that I had the power to prevent it. While in some cases struggling is avoidable, it is important to know that struggling is a part of life. I want to share this for those who are struggling, in this world we will continue to struggle, it is promised, but know that Jesus has overcome this world for us. We are able to have peace knowing that in our struggles we are covered by Him!

~ Maddie Smith, Wellesley ~

"Cast all your anxiety on him, because he cares for you."
1 Peter 5:7

This was a verse that I was reminded of last semester during FG, and it became such a source of strength for me during my senior year of college. Peter reminds us that we should never feel alone when we're anxious. There is a God who is available 24/7 that wants you to cast all anxieties on Him! No need to schedule a time to meet, or worry if you're oversharing. He is awaiting your prayers because He loves and cares for you more than you can imagine.

~ Jackson Fujimori, Northeastern ~

"Fear not, for I am with you; be not dismayed, for I am your God; I will strengthen you, I help you, I will uphold you with my righteous right hand"
Isaiah 41:10

I feel like it's so easy to be filled with anxiety during college. There are so many things to be excited about, but I know how easy it is to feel alone, purposeless, and unsuccessful when it seems like everyone around you is doing so well. This verse was one that really grounded me during my time in college and reminded me that there is someone greater who is always by my side, cheering me on, and helping me out. God has such a great plan for us, and Isaiah 41:10 reminds us that when we walk with God, He will give us the strength to succeed.

~ Jackson Fujimori, Northeastern ~
